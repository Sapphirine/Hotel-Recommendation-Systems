Conclusions

The two proposed models in our project, which combine popularity ranking, random forest, factorization machine and XGBoost, can significantly increase the accuracy rate of the hotel recommendation system. Such an improved recommender system can benefit online booking agencies such as Expedia, Booking and Kayak from at least two aspects:

Elevated Customer Experience.< The proposed models can offer more precise hotel recommendations to users. In this capacity, it can enhance user’s booking rates at Expedia and increase the booking efficiency by shortening users’ time spent on choosing hotels. With the elevated customer experience, such system is expected to broaden customer base and boost revenue.

A Better Demand Prediction Model. 70% of Expedia’s revenue comes from hoteliers(1). Expedia reserves a large number of hotel rooms at a steep discount from hoteliers and sells those rooms to users at a higher price. As a result, Expedia earns the price spread as its revenue. The key to successfully implement this strategy is to develop a precise demand prediction model which can predict the demand for each kind of hotels at the different time of the year. The proposed models can predict the likelihood of each hotel cluster being booked by different users. Should Expedia combine this model with its customer flow data, Expedia can develop a better demand prediction model and thus increases its market share and revenue.
